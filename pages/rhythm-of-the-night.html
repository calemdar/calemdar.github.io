Rhythm of The Night


Rhythm of the night is a rhythm based hack and slash type game build with Unreal Engine 4. You get to play as a local musician who went camping with your friends, until dangerous enemies started coming out of the shadows. You only have your campfire as your source of light, and you have to defend the tents, where your friends sleep, from the enemies. The main gameplay idea is to attack enemies in sync with the beat of the song.

I was the gameplay developer and designer for this game. I handled movement mechanics, design and integration of gameplay elements, and handled the interaction between the player and the AI. I created a Decal component that indicates where the cursor is, and switch the material of the component from green to red when hovering over an enemy. I created floating damage indicators, and polished environment assets. I also created a custom particle effect to go with the dashing ability, and to indicate temporary invulnerability.

In this project I learned a ton about the UE4 audio engine, visual and mechanical interactions between Actors in the game, and how to bring together separate features developed by different developers together without breaking the rest of the game. In this project we used Perforce as a source control software, and learned a lot about the differences in using Github vs Perforce.
