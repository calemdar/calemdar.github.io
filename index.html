About Me

As a traveler, I have always been amazed by how people can experience life differently in different cultures and under different conditions. I was born in Istanbul, Turkey and lived there until I started university at Worcester Polytechnic Institute; here I earned two bachelor’s degrees in Computer Science and Interactive Media and Game Development. My passion for games stemmed from watching my older brother play video games. My constant observation of what was going on in the game without actually having to think about what buttons to press gave me a whole new perspective of games and an understanding that everything on the screen has to serve a purpose. Seeing every little movement, every interaction, and every minuscule animation left me in awe of design and the ideas that went into creating an entirely new experience in a new form. I realized that I could design experiences to express an idea, or create a new immersive world that people could get lost in and live in; even if it doesn't actually physically exist. I could create a way to travel and escape with your mind through games, and as a traveler myself I know there are no experiences that can match those you get while traveling; and with games there is no limit to where you can go.
