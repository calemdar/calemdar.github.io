About Me

I am a traveler. I have always been amazed by how people can experience life differently in different cultures, under different conditions. This is because we all experience life differently. I was born in Istanbul, Turkey and lived there until I started university at Worcester Polytechnic Institute. I am pursuing my education in Video Game Development and Computer Science. I grew up watching my older brother play video games because we only had one computer. Constant observation of what is going on the screen without having to think about what buttons to press gave me an understanding of games that is different than something you can play for entertainment. Seeing every little movement, every interaction, every minuscule animation left me in awe of the design and thoughts that went into a game. I realized that I could design experiences to express an idea, or create a new world where the player can feel completely immersed in; even though it is not physically there. So people could go somewhere without actually being there.
